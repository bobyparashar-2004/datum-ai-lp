Frequently Asked Questions

What is Data Annotation or Labeling?
Data annotation refers to the process of labeling raw data so that AI models can learn from it. This is critical for supervised learning tasks like image recognition, speech-to-text conversion, and more.
How can I trust that my data will be safe?
We ensure all data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and comply with industry standards like HIPAA for healthcare data.
How do you ensure accurate labeling?
We implement multiple levels of validation, both manual and automated, to ensure the most accurate labeled data for you.
What types of data can you annotate?
We specialize in annotating various data types including images, videos, text, audio, and 3D point clouds. Our teams are trained across multiple domains and can handle specialized annotation requirements.
How quickly can you scale annotation services?
Our flexible workforce model allows us to rapidly scale from small pilot projects to enterprise-level annotation needs within days, not weeks. We maintain quality standards even during rapid scaling.
